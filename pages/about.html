Our Journey

2001

Establishment of Daskaya Pizza:

The journey began in 2001 when we opened our doors for the first time, serving delicious pizzas to our local community.

2005

Expansion of Menu:

In 2005, we expanded our menu to offer a wider variety of pizza options, introducing new flavors and toppings to satisfy diverse tastes.

2012

Introduction of Online Ordering:

Keeping up with technological advancements, we introduced online ordering in 2012, making it more convenient for our customers to enjoy our pizzas.

2017

Sustainable Practices:

In 2017, we embraced sustainable practices by sourcing high-quality, locally sourced ingredients and implementing eco-friendly packaging to reduce our environmental impact.

2020

Adapting to Challenges:

2020 posed challenges for everyone, and we adapted by enhancing our delivery services, offering contactless options, and supporting our community during tough times.

2023

Innovative Pizza Creations:

As of 2023, we continue to push the boundaries of pizza-making, introducing innovative and unique pizza creations that reflect our commitment to quality and creativity.
